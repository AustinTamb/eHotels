Search for Room

In order to search for a room, you must at least enter a from date and to date. With this, it is possible to only display available rooms. You may also decide to select to only search for a specific Hotel Chain. In addition, you can further narrow the results by typing in a city.

Once the results show up, all you need to do is click on the button to create a booking for the room. An employee at the hotel will take care of payment when you check in for the room. To cancel a booking, you may go to your profile where all your upcoming bookings will be displayed, and click on the icon.

In order to have the option to make a booking, you must be logged in.
